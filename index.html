Weibo Preview

Our publishing tool, KAWO, needed to show users how the content they were creating would be consumed, and since over 80% of Weibo usage is on mobile we decided to create a preview of the Weibo iOS app that updates as they create their post.

Images

For the purpose of this demo, images cannot be selected by the user but will be show according to the number of images specified.
